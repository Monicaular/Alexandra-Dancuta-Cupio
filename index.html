About us

Services

Basic/Standard Manicure

A basic or standard manicure includes trimming, shaping, and buffing nails, managing cuticles, applying cuticle oil, and a relaxing hand massage. The service ends with the application of standard nail polish.

Semi-permanent Manicure

A semi-permanent manicure includes nail preparation, trimming, shaping, and painting with base and polish lasting two weeks. Ideal for healthy, hard, and short nails.

Gel Manicure

A gel manicure involves the same process as a basic manicure, but with the application of a gel polish that is cured under a UV light. A topcoat is then applied for added protection and shine. This type of polish lasts longer than traditional polish and is a great option for nail art.

Polygel Manicure

Polygel nails are a type of manicure that combines the best qualities of gel and acrylic nails. They are durable, long-lasting and flexible, and are less damaging to the natural nail than other types of gel manicures. Polygel is cured under a UV light and is much lighter than traditional gel and acrylic nails.
