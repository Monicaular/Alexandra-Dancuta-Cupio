About us

At Alexandra Dancuta Cupio Ambassador, we believe in showcasing inner beauty through stunning nails. My salon is a haven of creativity, self-expression, and self-care.

I am Alexandra Dancuta, the founder of Alexandra Dancuta Cupio Ambassador. I'm passionate about nail art and helping you discover your own nail artistry. I provide classic and trendy designs with ongoing training.

Discover the perfect nail style at Alexandra Dancuta Cupio Ambassador. Join me for a journey of beauty and self-empowerment, one nail at a time.
